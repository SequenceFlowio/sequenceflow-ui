# C01: Google Search — high intent

**Budget:** €60/day initially, €55/day after retargeting launches
**Network:** Search only; disable Display expansion and search partners for the first test
**Location/language:** Netherlands; Dutch; people in or regularly in the location
**Bidding:** Maximise clicks with a conservative CPC ceiling until sign-up data is reliable; move to conversion bidding only after validated volume
**Landing:** `/for/webshops`

## Success criterion

Search terms remain commercially relevant and produce completed sign-ups that progress to a connected mailbox. Optimise away from broad “AI chatbot” intent even if those clicks are cheap.
